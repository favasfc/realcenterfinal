ABOUT US

Real centre is the part of a big group of companies which has established its presence in multiple kinds of businesses and proved to be successful through out its years of operations in the GCC. We have formed Real Center to be a venue in which people can connect with family, friends and colleagues, a place for socialising, meeting new people, exchanging ideas and above all enjoy and extract the fullness of life. Real feelings, Real moments, Real joy and all together the Reality of life. We host Parties, Birthday celebrations, Wedding functions, Business meetings, ... Big conferences, Association meetings and the list goes on. Real centre is the idea of our business owners who wanted to create a place of happiness filled with memories of the family and friends who gather here for the special occasions and celebrations. Real Center is meant for the people who aim to make their occasions a lovable and memorable experience as possible.
